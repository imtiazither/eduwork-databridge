# Claims and Maturity Boundaries

## Supported claims

- Blueprint Phases 0–14 are implemented in the v0.15.0 reference release.
- Public examples use deterministic synthetic data.
- The committed tests, benchmark, SBOMs, packages, documentation, and release evidence are reproducible under the recorded environment.
- The framework can be configured and extended for learning/workforce data workflows.

## Unsupported without additional evidence

- Production readiness for a specific company
- Regulatory or standards certification
- Company adoption, customer demand, or cost savings
- Improved completion, retention, productivity, employment, or equity outcomes
- Production-calibrated match probabilities or universal data-quality thresholds
- Passed Docker image, live PostgreSQL, registry signing, OIDC, or cloud-deployment checks unless those records are actually added

## Maturity label

`release-candidate / pre-production reference implementation`

The implementation blueprint is complete. Deployment-specific validation and a real, bounded partner pilot are the next evidence levels.
