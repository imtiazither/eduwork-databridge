# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is this an LMS or HRIS?

No. It is an interoperability, validation, identity-review, lineage, and publishing foundation that works alongside source systems.

## Does the project improve learning outcomes?

The public repository demonstrates technical and operational capabilities with synthetic data. Outcome improvement requires a real, appropriately designed evaluation.

## Is it compliant with FERPA, GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, or ISO 27001?

No blanket claim is made. Compliance depends on deployment, contracts, jurisdiction, policies, operations, and independent review.

## Are the match probabilities production-ready?

No. The public thresholds and estimated parameters are synthetic demonstration settings. Production requires representative labeled data, calibration, review capacity, monitoring, and approval.

## Can companies add proprietary adapters?

Yes. Keep proprietary code, schemas, credentials, and partner configuration in a private companion repository that depends on a tagged public core release.

## Why a modular monolith?

It is testable and operable by a small team while preserving package boundaries. Services can be extracted later only when scale or ownership justifies it.
