# Demo Walkthrough

The short walkthrough asset is stored at `docs/assets/eduwork-databridge-walkthrough.mp4`. It is a synthetic, slide-based product walkthrough rather than a recording of a real company system.

## Story

1. Fragmented HRIS, LMS, assessment, and credential sources enter through read-only connectors.
2. Original bytes become immutable checksummed snapshots.
3. Masked profiles expose structure and drift.
4. Bounded mapping rules translate fields into governed entities.
5. Validation produces transparent dimensions and quarantine evidence.
6. Deterministic and probabilistic matching preserve trusted-ID conflicts and human review.
7. Lineage links raw input, rules, marts, and masked exports.
8. Orchestration, telemetry, identity, authorization, audit, and retention make the workflow operable.

## Limitations shown in the video

- All data is synthetic.
- Probabilities and thresholds are demonstration values.
- No company deployment or outcome is implied.
- Docker/live PostgreSQL/registry-signing checks remain environment-specific.
